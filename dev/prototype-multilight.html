Tap any device → its controls open below (light → brightness + colour, TV → transport + volume, A/C → temperature + mode). Tap a zone → zoom + focus. Tap Lights control → lights get checkboxes (others dim); the panel stays inert until you check a light, then drives them as a group. Esc resets.
